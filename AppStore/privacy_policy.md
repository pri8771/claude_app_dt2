# Privacy Policy — Anjali

**Last updated: 28 June 2026**

Anjali is designed to be private by default. This policy explains, plainly, what
the app does and does not do with your information. The short version: **we don't
collect anything.**

## The short version

- Anjali collects **no personal data**.
- Anjali makes **no network requests** — it works entirely offline.
- There is **no account** and **no sign-in**.
- There is **no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking**.
- Everything you do stays **on your device**.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Anjali has no servers and no backend. We do not collect, transmit,
sell, or share any personal information. We have no ability to see how you use
the app.

## Information stored on your device

So the app can work and remember your preferences, some information is stored
**locally on your device only**, and never leaves it:

- **Preferences** (e.g. script choice, preferred prayer mode, chosen ishta
  devata, favourite moments, whether reminders are on) — stored in the system
  user-defaults on your device.
- **Your activity within the app** (prayers you complete and prayers you save)
  — stored locally using Apple's on-device SwiftData storage.

This information is not synced to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

## Network

Anjali makes **no network connections**. All prayer content ships inside the app
and is read locally. The app functions fully in airplane mode.

## Notifications

Anjali can show **local reminders** (e.g. a gentle morning or evening prompt).

- Notification permission is **only requested if you turn reminders on**. If you
  never enable reminders, the app never asks.
- Reminders are scheduled **locally on your device**. No push servers are
  involved and no information is sent anywhere.
- You can turn reminders off at any time in the app, or revoke notification
  permission in your device Settings.

## Children's privacy

Anjali does not collect data from anyone, including children. It contains no
ads, no tracking, and no external links that gather information.

## Third parties

Anjali includes **no third-party SDKs**, advertising networks, or analytics
tools. No data is shared with third parties because none is collected.

## Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change (for example, if an optional sync
feature is added in a future version), this policy will be updated and the
"Last updated" date above will change. Any new data use would be clearly
explained and, where appropriate, made opt-in.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the support address listed on our
support page: `https://anjali.app/support`.
