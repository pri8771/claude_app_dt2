# Anjali — Hero Prayers (audio recording priority)

The first recitations to record, chosen from the current 22-prayer seed.

**Criteria:** high spiritual resonance · broad everyday use · short duration ·
clear/approachable pronunciation. Daily anchors and the most universally
recognised mantras come first, since they surface most often on Today.

**v1 plan:** record the **20 heroes** below; leave **2** long dhyāna ślokas as
**text-only** for v1 (every deity still has audio coverage without them).
