# Anjali — Marketing & Launch Plan

## Executive Summary
Anjali is positioning for organic growth through authentic cultural channels, word-of-mouth, and targeted outreach to Hindu communities globally. No paid advertising planned for MVP launch; focus on community alignment and genuine user resonance.

## Target Audience

### Primary
- Hindu practitioners aged 18-65 with active spiritual practice
- - Looking for authentic, respectful prayer companions
  - - Comfortable with technology, iOS users
    - - Motivated by tradition, cultural preservation, and convenience
     
      - ### Secondary
      - - Interfaith practitioners interested in Hindu wisdom
        - - Heritage learners reconnecting with culture
          - - Yoga practitioners seeking complementary spiritual tools
           
            - ### Geographic Priority
            - - United States (40% of target users)
              - - India (20%)
                - - UK, Canada, Australia (15%)
                  - - Diaspora communities worldwide (25%)
                   
                    - ## Messaging Pillars
                   
                    - ### 1. Authenticity
                    - "A sacred pause, not a session" — Anjali respects the brevity and intentionality of Hindu prayer traditions. No gamification, no streaks, just genuine spiritual practice.
                   
                    - ### 2. Accessibility
                    - Breaks down barriers to prayer: Available offline, in multiple scripts, across prayer traditions. For the busy professional, the traveling practitioner, the learning student.
                   
                    - ### 3. Cultural Integrity
                    - Built with Hindu scholars, content advisors, and practitioners. Represents traditions with accuracy and respect, not appropriation.
                   
                    - ### 4. Simplicity
                    - Minimal, elegant design reflects the focus on the practice itself, not the interface.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - ## Risk Mitigation
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - ### Risk: Low initial awareness
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Mitigation:** Focus on niches with high cultural alignment (temples, cultural centers, yoga communities) rather than broad appeal. Authentic within communities > broad awareness.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - ### Risk: Negative reviews from inauthentic positioning
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Mitigation:** Ensure core positioning is genuine before launch. Have cultural advisors review all marketing materials.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - ### Risk: Competing with free prayer apps
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Mitigation:** Differentiate on authenticity, offline-first design, and cultural integrity vs. free ad-supported alternatives.
